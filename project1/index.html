해녀

海女

Haenyeo

About

Literally, the word “hae” means “sea” and “nyeo” means “female”, and haenyeo are female divers in the Korean province of Jeju whose livelihood consists of harvesting a variety of mollusks, seaweed, and other sea life from the ocean. Known for their independent spirit, iron will and determination, the haenyeo are representative of the semi-matriarchal family structure of Jeju.
